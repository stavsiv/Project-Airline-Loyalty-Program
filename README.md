# Project-Airline Loyalty Program

**Project Overview**
This project analyzes customer loyalty data from a Canadian airline to evaluate the effectiveness of a targeted marketing campaign conducted between February and April 2018.

The main objective is to assess whether the campaign not only increased customer enrollment, but also attracted high-quality customers who contribute to long-term business value.

To achieve this, the analysis focuses on several key areas:

Campaign Impact on Activity:
Evaluating changes in flight activity and customer enrollment during the campaign period compared to previous years.
Customer Retention & Churn:
Measuring post-campaign cancellation rates to understand whether newly acquired customers remained active over time.
Customer Engagement:
Analyzing average flights and travel distance per customer to assess usage and engagement levels.
Customer Value (CLV):
Comparing customer lifetime value between campaign-acquired users and baseline customers.
Post-Campaign Performance (Summer Impact):
Assessing whether the campaign had a lasting effect by analyzing flight activity during the summer months following the campaign, indicating sustained engagement beyond the promotional period.

The project combines data analysis and visualization (Tableau & Python) to provide insights into both short-term growth and long-term customer quality, supporting data-driven decisions for future marketing strategies.

**Key Metrics**
Net New Customers (Post-Cancellation) – number of campaign-acquired users who remained active
Cancellation Rate – percentage of campaign users who churned over time
Customer Engagement – average flights and travel distance per user
Customer Value (CLV) – long-term revenue contribution of customers

**Key Insights**
The campaign led to a significant increase in flight activity compared to previous years
Customers acquired through the campaign demonstrated higher engagement levels
Despite some cancellations, the majority of users remained active, indicating strong customer retention
Campaign users showed slightly higher lifetime value, suggesting positive long-term impact

**🛠 Tools & Technologies**
Tableau – interactive dashboards and KPI visualization
Python (Pandas, Matplotlib) – data analysis and advanced metrics

**Outcome**
The analysis shows that the campaign not only increased customer acquisition but also attracted high-quality, engaged users who contribute to sustained business growth.

**Key Takeaway**
The 2018 campaign represents high-quality growth — successfully combining increased activity with strong customer value and retention.
